## 🔭 Web3 Builder Pulse — 2026-06-22

### 📊 Today's Snapshot

Today's Web3 development landscape shows a strong focus on robust infrastructure and enhanced user experiences. Security and interoperability remain paramount, with significant activity in bridging solutions and core blockchain implementations. The push for decentralized social applications continues to gain traction, hinting at the next wave of mainstream adoption.

### 🔥 Top 3 Projects to Watch

1.  **unionlabs/union (⭐73950) [Rust]:** This trust-minimized, zero-knowledge bridging protocol is a critical piece of infrastructure. Its emphasis on censorship resistance and high security is essential for the growing DeFi ecosystem and the future of cross-chain communication. The continued stellar growth signals strong developer conviction in its architecture.
2.  **FuelLabs/fuel-core (⭐56995) [Rust]:** As a full node implementation of the Fuel v2 protocol, this repo highlights the ongoing innovation in Layer 2 scaling solutions. Fuel's focus on performance and developer experience is key to unlocking the next generation of dApps, and its traction indicates a maturing modular blockchain ecosystem.
3.  **slymnoyann/hey-1 (⭐29383) [TypeScript]:** This decentralized and permissionless social media app built on Lens Protocol represents a tangible step towards Web3-native social networking. Its strong showing reflects a growing desire for user-owned data and censorship-resistant social platforms, moving beyond theoretical concepts to practical applications.

### 📈 Tech Trends Rising

*   **Rust:** Dominates the top infrastructure projects, underscoring its importance for high-performance, secure blockchain development (Union, Fuel, Solana).
*   **Zero-Knowledge (ZK) Proofs:** As seen in Union's bridging protocol, ZK technology is central to building more secure and private Web3 applications.
*   **Decentralized Social Media (DeSoc):** "hey-1"'s popularity points to a rising trend in building user-centric social platforms on protocols like Lens.
*   **Solidity & Foundries:** While not explicitly trending high in stars today, the inclusion of DeFiHackLabs (Solidity) and the constant mention in educational content (smartcontractkit) indicates ongoing core development and security auditing importance.

### 💀 What's Cooling Down

While core Ethereum development (go-ethereum) remains steady, the absence of major trending repositories focused on pure Layer 1 Ethereum application development suggests a shift towards Layer 2 solutions and entirely new L1 architectures. Similarly, while general blockchain educational content persists, highly specific introductory Etheruem courses might be seeing less standalone trending interest compared to the underlying infrastructure or newer L1s.

### 🧠 Builder Insight

Focus on building resilient, interoperable infrastructure. The current trending repositories point to a "build the roads" mentality – secure bridges, scalable L2s, and robust core protocols are the foundation upon which the next wave of mainstream dApps will be built.
